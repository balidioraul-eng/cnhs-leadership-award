Leadership Award Nomination Form

This form is designed for Compostela National High School and aligned with the Leadership Award under DepEd Order No. 36, s. 2016, which recognizes learners who show exemplary ability to lead, motivate others, and organize school or community-benefiting initiatives.

Instructions:
1. Enter the nominee name in CAPITAL LETTERS using this format: SURNAME, GIVEN NAME, MI.
2. Adviser and peer entries are separate. Choose only one rating type per submission.
3. Only 5 peer ratings are allowed per nominee.
4. A nominee may appear many times in submissions, but the dashboard will combine all identical nominee names into one averaged summary rating.
5. Grade 10 nominees may include accomplishments from Grades 7 to 10. Grade 12 nominees may include accomplishments from Grades 11 to 12.
6. You may attach up to 5 files/documents.

Weighted Criteria

Percentage-based scoring for Leadership Award evaluation.

Leadership and Initiative – 25%
Influence and Motivation – 25%
Responsibility and Accountability – 20%
Teamwork and Collaboration – 15%
Contribution to School/Community – 15%
Score per criterion: 0–100%. Total weighted score is also shown in percentage.
